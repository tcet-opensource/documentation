Fill My Cycle Website

This is the official documentation of the Fill My Cycle , maintained by the TCET Open Source Documentation team.

The main purpose of this website is to calculate the performance of students and assess them as per the marks scored by them in various subjects.

Breakdown of website​

Cycle Defining section​

Here students enter there marks scored by them in different sections of a subject such as assignments, practicals, vivas, tests etc. average of these marks are calculated.

Cycle displaying section​

Here the marks of students are broken down into various parameter and then represented in the form of tick marks in various cell representing certain score.
As you see in the image below:

Cycle Result Section​

Here the marks of students are calculated as per represented in the Cycle displayint section in the respective cells. Later the average of these marks are calculated and hence students get to know about their over all performance.
